iCleaner Privacy Policy

At iCleaner, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your information when you use our mobile application ("iCleaner," "the App," "our App") on Android and iOS devices. By using iCleaner, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Quick Summary: iCleaner is designed with privacy-first principles. We do not sell your data to third parties. All cleaning and optimization operations happen locally on your device. Your photos, videos, contacts, and files remain private and are never uploaded to our servers without your explicit consent.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use iCleaner, you may provide certain information directly to us:

  • Account Information: If you create an account (optional), we collect your email address, username, and password (encrypted).
  • Vault Content: Files, photos, videos, and documents you choose to store in the secure vault remain encrypted and stored locally on your device. We do not have access to the contents of your vault.
  • Contact Information: When you use our contact cleanup feature, we temporarily access your contacts list to identify duplicates and merge entries. This data is processed locally and never transmitted to our servers.
  • Support Communications: When you contact us at phone.icleaner@gmail.com, we collect your email address and the content of your message to provide support.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

To improve the App's functionality and user experience, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: We collect device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information, and device language settings.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, including features used, cleaning operations performed, storage space recovered, and app performance metrics.
  • Storage Analysis Data: To perform cleaning operations, we analyze your device storage, including file types, sizes, creation dates, and locations. This analysis happens locally on your device.
  • Crash Reports and Diagnostics: We collect crash reports and error logs to identify and fix technical issues. These reports may include device state information at the time of the crash but do not include personal content.
  • Analytics Data: We use analytics tools to understand app usage patterns, popular features, and areas for improvement. This data is aggregated and anonymized.

1.3 Permissions We Request

iCleaner requests the following permissions to function properly. You can manage these permissions in your device settings:

  • Storage Permission: Required to scan, analyze, and clean junk files, cache, temporary files, and manage media files.
  • Contacts Permission: Required for the contact cleanup feature to identify and merge duplicate contacts. Access is only granted if you use this feature.
  • Photo/Media Permission: Required to identify duplicate photos, compress images/videos, and manage your media library.
  • Email Access (Optional): If you use the email cleaner feature, we request permission to access your email metadata (subject lines, sender information, dates) to help you clean your inbox. We never read the content of your emails.
  • Notification Permission: To send you alerts about storage issues, cleaning recommendations, and app updates.
  • Biometric Permission: For the secure vault feature, we request fingerprint or face recognition permissions to protect your private files.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • Perform junk file cleaning, cache removal, and storage optimization
  • Identify and remove duplicate files, photos, and videos
  • Merge duplicate contacts and organize your address book
  • Compress photos and videos to save storage space
  • Secure your private files in the encrypted vault
  • Provide email inbox cleaning recommendations
  • Generate storage analysis reports and cleaning suggestions

2.2 App Improvement and Development

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve existing features
  • Develop new features based on user needs and feedback
  • Identify and fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues
  • Optimize AI algorithms for better cleaning recommendations
  • Conduct A/B testing to improve user experience

2.3 Communication and Support

  • Respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • Send important updates about the App, including security updates
  • Provide tips and recommendations for better phone optimization
  • Notify you about new features and improvements

2.4 Security and Fraud Prevention

  • Protect against unauthorized access and security threats
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations and law enforcement requests

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Data Processing

iCleaner is designed with a "local-first" approach. All cleaning operations, file analysis, and optimizations happen directly on your device. Your photos, videos, documents, and contacts are not uploaded to our servers during normal app operations.

3.2 Secure Vault

Files stored in the iCleaner vault are encrypted using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. The encryption keys are stored securely on your device and are protected by your device's biometric authentication or PIN code. We do not have access to your vault encryption keys or the contents of your vault.

3.3 Data Transmission Security

When data must be transmitted to our servers (such as crash reports or usage analytics), we use secure HTTPS connections with TLS encryption to protect data in transit.

3.4 Cloud Backup (Optional)

If you choose to enable cloud backup for your vault (premium feature), your encrypted files are uploaded to secure cloud storage. Even in cloud storage, your files remain encrypted with keys only you possess. We cannot decrypt or access your backed-up files.

3.5 Data Retention

  • Usage Data and Analytics: Retained for up to 24 months for analysis and improvement purposes
  • Crash Reports: Retained for up to 12 months to identify and fix recurring issues
  • Account Information: Retained until you delete your account
  • Support Communications: Retained for up to 36 months for customer service purposes
  • Vault Content: Retained until you delete the files or uninstall the app. Cloud backups are retained according to your subscription settings

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

iCleaner does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Your privacy is not for sale.

4.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the App. These providers have access only to the information necessary to perform their functions and are contractually obligated to protect your data:

  • Analytics Providers: We use analytics services (such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics) to understand app usage. These providers collect anonymized usage data.
  • Cloud Storage Providers: If you enable cloud backup, encrypted vault files are stored with reputable cloud storage providers (AWS, Google Cloud).
  • Crash Reporting Services: We use crash reporting tools (such as Crashlytics) to identify and fix technical issues.
  • Customer Support Tools: We use email services and support platforms to respond to your inquiries.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities, including:
